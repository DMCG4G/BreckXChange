Send Us Your Gear

Breckenridge Bike Exchange was designed for Summit County residents to have an easy and no hassle way to sell their gear. We strive to give back to the community, help people find the gear they want, and partner with great causes like the Go4Graham Foundation.

Receive An Offer

Once you send us a message with what you are selling, you will receive an offer range pending the quality of the gear. We try to be quick with our offers, but do our best to respond with an offer in 48 hours. The more information you provide, the better!

Get Paid

Once we agree on an offer range, we will schedule pick up or drop off of your gear. After this we will give you a final offer. You will receive a fair offer for your goods. You'll have the choice between being paid out in cash or credit towards trading up in our store.
