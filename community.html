Community Comes First

At Breckenridge Bike Exchange, we are more than just a gear shop or a means to sell/buy gear. We believe in fostering a welcoming community of cyclists, skiers and snowboarders, and outdoor enthusiasts! Stop by the shop to see what we are all about. We host clinics, weekly group rides, and even have a small book club!

Not Sure Your Skill Level?

No problem! We have ride leaders who are certified who will divide our group up into separate groups. Both groups are no drop rides and we all will meet back up at the shop for post ride banter and beverages! Please leave your dogs at home though. We love dogs, but not every dog is a good trail dog.
