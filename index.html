Breckenridge Bike Exchange

Breckenridge Bike Exchange is a locally-owned, outdoor gear shop in Breckenridge, CO. We are known for having Breckenridge’s largest selection of used outdoor gear and clothing. Breckenridge Bike Exchange also offers new items, gear rentals, mobile bike repair, and guided mountain bike tours. You’ll find gear for mountain biking, road biking, gravel biking, skiing, snowboarding, and other general outdoor fun. In addition, Breckenridge Bike Exchange fosters a strong community of outdoor enthusiasts with exciting events and unique collaborations throughout the year..
